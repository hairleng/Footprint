About Footprint

Footprint is a travel journal app to document your trip. Travelers can use it to create digital logs, which enhance your text with photos and maps marking all of the places you visited, with the option to keep entries private or share them with family and friends. The app also allows users to share their logs and find logs posted by other travelers all over the world. Wanna have a try? Sign up now!

Developer Journal

We started Footprint as a course project for 17-637 in 2021 Spring and got a bunch of experience in Django, Ajax and front-end. We took advantage of Codepen, Bootstrap, Stackoverflow, and other useful online resources. Thank you to everyone who selflessly share their programming knowledge. It's already a great journey for us!
